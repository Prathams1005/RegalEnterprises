Our History

Our Company

Regal Enterprises is a leading civil construction and maintenance company serving Vasai-Virar and Nallasopara since 2000. Founded by Mr. Iqbal Pathan with the vision of providing quality services and lasting values for clients, Regal Enterprises is built on pillars of trust, transparency and timely delivery. With a team of experienced engineers, supervisors, certified technicians and skilled workmen, we have successfully delivered hundreds of projects across segments - residential, commercial, corporate and industrial. Our capability encompasses the full spectrum of new construction, repairs, renovations, maintenance and facelift services. We handle everything from planning to execution to deliver your projects on time and on budget. At Regal Enterprises, your satisfaction is our biggest reward. We believe in forging long-term relationships by exceeding your expectations. Our commitment to impeccable service quality and customer centricity makes us the trusted construction partner in the region. We are grateful for the continuous patronage of our clients over the past two decades. Get in touch with us for your next project. We assure you the best of services and look forward to a mutually rewarding association
